OUR SUITES

A life of comfort, design and luxury. With a total of 335 rooms, Mombasa Whitesands Beach Resort & Spa offers an array of dedicated choices for every guest. Our guests will always find the most tailored option to make their stay in Mombasa the greatest one of them all.


Mombasa Whitesands has 3 rooms that have been adapted for guests with special needs / who are physically challenged. Our staff will endeavour to assist guests with special needs as much as they possibly can, and will attempt to anticipate your needs.
